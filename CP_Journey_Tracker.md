### Contest Strategy
1. **Read A → Solve A**
2. **Read B → Solve B**
3. **Do NOT Panic.** Accuracy > Speed initially.

### The Upsolve Protocol (CRUCIAL)
**Never skip this step. Growth happens here.**

- [ ] **Scenario 1:** Solved A, B, C in contest? → **Try D the next day.**
- [ ] **Scenario 2:** Failed B? → **Solve B and C the next day.**
- [ ] **Scenario 3:** Couldn't solve C? → **Solve C and D the next day.**
