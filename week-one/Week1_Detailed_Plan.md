## 💡 Tips for Week 1

1. **Don't overthink 800-rated problems.** Most are straightforward.
2. **If stuck > 15 mins, read the editorial.** Learn the pattern, move on.
3. **Track your time.** This helps identify weak areas.
4. **Focus on clean code.** Build good habits early.
5. **Don't skip the virtual contest.** Even if scared, participate.
